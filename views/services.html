What We Do

Providing transportation to seniors and people with visual impairments

We operate within Gainesville, Georgia

CHARACTERISTICS OF THE SERVICE

Available 7 days a week, 24 hours a day for any purpose
No money is exchanged in the vehicle, and riders pre-fund a personal transportation account
Rides are provided in private automobiles by trained drivers, no vans or buses
We welcome members that are 60 years and older or who have disabilities
Rides may be booked rides with discounts
Riders may travel alone or with others, with 20% discounts for shared rides
Community based affiliates are supported by private, rather than public resources
ITNs are non-profit, keeping fares affordable
Not "just a taxi" - drivers provide arm-through-arm, door-to-door service and help with packages
The CarTrade™ program allows you to trade your car for rides
The HealthyMiles™ program allows businesses and health cares providers to participate

Give us a call to find out if you live within our operating radius

We are a membership based organization with an annual membership fee where two of the members over 60 years of age or adults with visual impairment 21 years and older live in the same household can subscribe.

ITNLanier provides its members with a unique rider service

Dignified

The Independent Transportation Network® (ITN) is dignified. Older adults who join ITNLanier become dues-paying members of a non-profit organization committed to their independence and mobility. When they pick up the telephone to schedule a ride, they are not asking a favor. The transition from the driver's seat to the passenger seat is never easy - for the drivers or for their families - but it is infinitely easier when there is a viable transportation alternative that keeps the older adult in control.

Consumer Oriented

The ITNLanier service is also consumer oriented. We provide excellent customer service with the arm-through-arm, door-to-door standard that sets us apart from taxis. Like using one's own automobile, the ITNLanier service is available 24/7 and gives customers the flexibility to schedule their rides in advance or as needed, as well as the option to ride alone or with others, as they please, to destinations of their choice.
