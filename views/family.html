Family Involvement

How can family members help?

Family members care deeply when a parent, grandparent or spouse struggles with safe driving.

With ITN's door-through-door, arm-through-arm service, older family members are as safe as if their own children were there to open the door and carry their packages.

Adult children can help manage their parents' Personal Transportation Account™ by having the monthly statements sent directly to them. There is also a Gift Certificate program to help pay for rides. It's a great idea for Mother's Day, Father's Day, birthdays, holidays, and other special occasions.

Adult children who live in any community with an ITN affiliate can earn transportation credits by volunteering to drive seniors. Because ITNAmerica is a nationally affiliated system, their parents may use those credits to pay for rides in the community where they live, even if they are located thousands of miles away.

Family members may also support The Independent Transportation Network® by joining as dues paying members (even if you don't qualify to ride), donating a car, making a donation, or helping with community fundraisers.

Gift Certificates

A gift certificate for transportation is an original gift that can be used to take fun trips or necessary ones. They are a great way to help your parent or friend fund their Personal Transportation Account™. Celebrate Mother's Day, birthdays, and special holidays with a gift certificate in any amount, sent in a beautiful card by ITNLanier. Call or e-mail to order.

If you are trying to encourage safe, alternative transportation for your loved one, consider a gift certificate to pay for membership and their first ride. Our trained drivers will transport your loved one with special care -- opening doors, carrying packages, and offering friendly assistance as needed.
