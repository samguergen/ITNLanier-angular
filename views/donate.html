ITNLanier is a nonprofit working to ensure seniors and the visually impaired in Gainesville have access to dignified transportation options. We help families stay connected and secure independence for members of our community, helping adult children minimize absenteeism from work, businesses remain profitable, and seniors begin a new chapter in their lives that replaces dependence and isolation with independence and mobility.

Help us keep our community moving. Together we are opening more than car doors.
ITNLanier is a 501(c)(3) tax-exempt organization.
